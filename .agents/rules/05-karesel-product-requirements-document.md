# KARESEL PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

KARESEL

## Product Category

AI-powered educational pixel coding and worksheet generation platform.

## Product Description

KARESEL transforms ordinary images into age-appropriate visual coding activities for children aged 5–10.

The platform combines:

* Computer vision.
* Educational psychology.
* Artificial intelligence.
* Image processing.
* Child-centered design.

The objective is not to create the most realistic pixel art.

The objective is to create the most effective learning activity.

---

# 2. Product Vision

KARESEL aims to become the world's leading educational pixel coding platform for primary education.

The platform helps children develop:

* Computational thinking.
* Problem solving.
* Pattern recognition.
* Visual intelligence.
* Attention and concentration.
* Creativity.
* Fine motor skills.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Teachers spend valuable time preparing educational coding worksheets.

Existing pixel art tools generally have several limitations:

* They are designed for artists instead of teachers.
* They focus on visual similarity rather than educational usability.
* They do not consider children's developmental levels.
* They require manual adjustment.
* They provide no educational quality evaluation.

KARESEL solves these problems through intelligent educational automation.
